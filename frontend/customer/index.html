What is 3D Printing?

Additive manufacturing that creates physical objects from digital files, layer by layer

📐

Design

Start with a 3D model file (STL, OBJ, etc.) created in Blender, Fusion 360, or download from Thingiverse.

⚙️

Slice & Print

Your file is sliced into thin layers and built up by the printer using melted filament or UV-cured resin.

✨

Deliver

After printing and post-processing, your item is quality-checked, packaged, and delivered to your door.
